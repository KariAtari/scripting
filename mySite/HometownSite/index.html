Medicine Hat is the sixth largest city in Alberta and is a very well-rounded place to live for people of all ages. From its warmer temperatures in the summertime, it Is known as one of the sunniest cities in Canada with an estimated 330 days of sunshine. There is a lot of culture and history, even in the most unexpected places. With a great mix of modern and classic architecture, downtown streets are lined with antique buildings. For example, The Medicine Hat Courthouse was built in 1920.

Residents will have easy access to entertainment and activities that suit all ages throughout the year, as it is a wonderful place to start a family or to retire to. The bus transit system is reliable and there is an airport with a shuttle service as well.
