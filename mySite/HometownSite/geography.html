Medicine Hat can be found in the corner of Alberta in Western Canada, meaning it is on the Trans-Canada Highway and along the South Saskatchewan River. Though what may seem like just flat prairies and grass, Medicine Hat houses a lot of coulees. They provide remarkable views of the city and the hillside slopes that can be found within it. There are a variety of trees lining the parks and trails. It can range from towering pine trees to big leafy trees. Autumn is always inviting with its warm orange hues that announce the colder weather is soon approaching.

With the sight of sparkling fluffy snow covering all the hills, winter is just as beautiful.
